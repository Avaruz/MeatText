Share your location with anyone. No matter what device, operating system or connection you share.

MeatText is platform agnostic- when sharing your location, it defaults to the recipients native mapping app (Apple maps, Google Maps). Your friend does not have MeatText? No problem, the connection will still be made.

It's incredibly more accurate than other services. Accuracy is everything.

Apple Maps sends a location that is tied to the nearest restaurant, business or center of a park. MeatText is tied to latitude and longitude.

Want to Tweet the location of the conference to the masses? Or E-Mail your grandma the location of the park bench?

We've got you covered. With MeatText you can text, Tweet or E-Mail your location.

Privacy

Who is my location shared with?

Your location is shared with anyone you share it with. If you tweet it, it will be made public (as long as your tweets are public). The location you share is just one location in time, I.E. it does not update as you move.

How long does the link to the location last?

Indefinitely

Can I restrict who sees my location?

If you prefer that someone does not see your location, don't share it with them.
